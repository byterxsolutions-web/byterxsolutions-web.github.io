Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Introduction

ByteRX Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FocusDose mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using FocusDose, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use FocusDose, you may provide:

  • Profile data: Your name, wake-up time, bedtime, and preference settings that you enter during onboarding.
  • Task data: Custom task names, sub-steps, scheduled tasks, and task completion records that you create within the App.
  • Appointment data: Appointment names, times, and recurrence settings you add to your schedule.
  • Check-in responses: Your responses to periodic focus check-ins (e.g., "on track" or "need help").

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Usage data: App interaction patterns, feature usage frequency, and session duration to help us improve the App experience.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information (payments are handled entirely by the Google Play Store).
  • We do not collect health or medical information. FocusDose is a productivity tool, not a medical device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core features (task scheduling, check-ins, task breakdown, gamification).
  • Generate your personalized daily schedule based on your preferences and appointments.
  • Send local notifications (e.g., task reminders, check-in nudges) that you have opted into.
  • Track your gamification progress (XP, levels, streaks, achievements) locally on your device.
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the App.

Data Storage & Security

FocusDose stores all your data locally on your device using encrypted storage (MMKV with encryption). Your data is not uploaded to our servers.

We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • On-device encrypted storage for all task data, preferences, and gamification progress.
  • HTTPS encryption for any data transmitted to third-party services.
  • No server-side database of user data maintained by ByteRX Solutions.

Third-Party Services

FocusDose integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: Handles subscription purchases and trial management. Google's handling of payment data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. FocusDose contains no advertisements.

Subscription & Billing

FocusDose offers a subscription model with a 7-day free trial. All payment processing is handled by the Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing address. Subscription management (cancellation, renewal) is handled through Google Play.

Children's Privacy

FocusDose is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Data access & deletion: Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications at any time through your device's notification settings or within the App's preferences.
  • Subscription: You can cancel your subscription at any time through the Google Play Store. Your app will continue to work until the end of the current billing period.

Data Retention

Your task data, preferences, and gamification progress are stored on your device for as long as you use the App. We do not retain copies of your data on our servers. When you uninstall FocusDose, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
